ABOUT US

Martin Engineering is a leading provider of weigh bridges and electronic weighing scales, with a rich history of engineering and trading expertise. Founded in Ghaziabad, India, we are a family-owned business that has been serving customers for generations. Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and customer-focused approach have made us a trusted and reliable supplier in the industry.
At Martin Engineering, we understand that accurate weight measurement is essential for many industries, including transportation, logistics, manufacturing, and agriculture. That's why we work tirelessly to provide our customers with the best products and services in the market. Our team of experienced engineers and technicians work closely with customers to understand their specific needs and provide customized solutions that meet their requirements. Whether you're looking for a simple weigh bridge or a complex electronic weighing system, we have the expertise and experience to deliver.
We are proud to offer a wide range of high-quality weigh bridges and electronic weighing scales, all backed by our industry-leading customer support. Our goal is to ensure that every customer is completely satisfied with their purchase, and that's why we offer comprehensive after-sales support, including installation, maintenance, and repair services. Our team is always available to assist with any questions or concerns, ensuring that your equipment remains in top condition for years to come.
At Martin Engineering, we believe in doing business the right way. We are committed to providing our customers with the highest-quality products and services, at competitive prices, and with a focus on sustainability. Whether you're looking for a long-term partner or just need a one-time solution, we are here to help. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help your business.

FULL STEEL PLATFORM WEIGHBRIDGES - PITLESS TYPE

Full Steel Pitless type:

This type of weighbridge / Truckscale is most suitable where the excavation work for foundation is difficult or construction of pit is expensive. Since the structure is above ground level, vehicles can approach the weighbridge only from two directions where the ramps are provided. This type of weighbridge requires more space.

Benefits:

• Costly Pit construction is eliminated.
• Platform above ground level- No water logging during rainy season
• Problem of pit maintenance is eliminated.
• Easy maintenance since all parts accessible above ground level.
